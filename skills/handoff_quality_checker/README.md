# Handoff Quality Checker
**ID:** `handoff_quality_checker`
**Category:** Design Ops & QA

## Overview
Acts as the final gatekeeper before Engineering begins work. Ensures the designer has satisfied every item on an engineering-provided "Definition of Ready" checklist. 

## When to Trigger
- **Phase 5 (Validation & Review)**
- The absolute last step before a feature moves from "In Design" to "Ready for Dev".
